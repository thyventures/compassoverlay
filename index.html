Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Compass Overlay

How accurate is the compass in the app?
The compass in our app uses your device's built-in magnetometer to provide highly accurate directional information. For even more precision, you can manually calibrate the compass or input exact directional data if you know it.
Can I save and share the images with compass overlay?
Yes! After adding a compass overlay to your image, you can easily save it to your device or share it directly via messaging apps, email, or social media platforms. This makes it perfect for real estate agents who want to include directional information in their listings.
How does the sun compass feature work?
The sun compass feature uses advanced algorithms to calculate the sun's position relative to your property at different times of the year. This helps you understand seasonal variations in sun exposure, which is crucial for assessing factors like natural lighting, heat management, and energy efficiency.
Is Compass Overlay available offline?
Yes, most of the app's core functionality works offline. However, some features like location-based services may require an internet connection for optimal accuracy.
Can I use Compass Overlay for feng shui analysis?
Absolutely! Compass Overlay is widely used by feng shui practitioners and enthusiasts. The precise directional measurements help determine the exact orientation of a property, which is essential for accurate feng shui analysis.
